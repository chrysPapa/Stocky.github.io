Stock Market analytics

And general news

I have been an active trader in the stock market for two years now. As a new investor, I did not have sufficient knowledge of this domain and found it exceptionally hard to find any adequate data. So, I am aiming to create a website that will provide the user's information on their searches by collecting data on the stock market and comparing them. The data will be simplified and more accurate us it will compare more than one sources and present to the users some analytics. The biggest reason behind my idea is to help traders by producing more accurate information and making their life bit easier us they could save some time by searching and comparing pieces of information on their own.
